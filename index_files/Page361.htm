OUR MISSION

Our mission is to inspire kids to investigate and explore a world of Science and Technology. We believe that it will help them to choose an interesting profession in the future.

 

ABOUT US

We are a group of volunteers, teachers and parents who would like to introduce our kids to a great world of science, by creating an unforgettable experience of the team work, while building a unique set of skills. In 2015 we founded a small non-profit technical club which allows our children to complete a few Electronic and Programming projects. We feel like we have grown up enough and would be able to take a part in a serious robotic competition. Our team cannot wait to start building real warrior bots.
